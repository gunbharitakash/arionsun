Who We Are

A Legacy of Quality

Arion Sun is a premier export house engaged in the trading and marketing of agricultural produce. We bridge the gap between Indian farmers and international buyers, ensuring the authentic taste and nutritional value of our commodities remain intact.


We specialize in Indian Spices, Grains, Sugar, and Pulses. Our network spans across Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Bangladesh, UAE, and Singapore. As a B2B trader, we are highly specialized in maintaining stocks and distributing them efficiently to manufacturers and exporters.
